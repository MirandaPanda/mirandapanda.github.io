Miranda Cavanagh

UX Designer | Web Developer

I am creative, perceptive, honest, and analytical. I can speak to humans and computers in their respective languages. I believe that a design is only as good as its user's experience. I beleve in collaboration and the importance in cross-functionality in the creation of a product. I've held many public-facing roles, which have allowed me to be keenly aware of people and their needs. I have years of varied experience in qualitative and quantitative human analysis, research, and creative problem solving. My goal is to create a positive impact on a product or business by creating an effective, successful user experience. I focus on bridging creative problem solving with business goals through human understanding and interactive design.

My Process

Communication. I work closely with the client to identify their problem. I ask questions, create personas, and perform research and analysis. This close communication remains constant throughout my process.

Design. I solve pain points with creative solutions by targeting users' experience. I use sketches, notes, lists, flow charts, wireframes, mockups, prototypes, etc. to do so.

Testing. I understand and work with users to create functional, purposeful design. I use tree testing, user feedback, beta testing, focus groups, etc., before, after and while changes are made to a product. This is iterative and constant.

Production. This is not the final stage of a product. Through discovery of new information, ideas, or testing results, implementation is ongoing, iterative, and improved.
